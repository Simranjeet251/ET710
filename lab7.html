Heels Collection

Discover our stylish heels collection.

There are many types of heels, including low heels, mid heels, high heels, platform heels, and more. Heel height Low heels: Heels that are up to 3 inches tall Mid heels: Heels that are 3–4 inches tall High heels: Heels that are 4 inches or taller Heel style Stilettos: Long, thin heels that taper to a point Platform heels: Thick-soled shoes with heels that are higher than the sole Wedges: Shoes with triangular soles that function as both the heel and the shoe sole Square heels: Heels with a solid, flat base and sharp corners Block heels: Thicker and chunkier heels that are inspired by retro fashion

Women's Shoes Collection

Explore fashionable women's shoes.

Women's shoes are a type of footwear designed for women, and come in a variety of styles, including high heels, sandals, boots, and sneakers. Features Size Women's shoes are generally narrower and have a more tapered toe box than men's shoes. Style Women's shoes often have higher heels and more decorative elements than men's shoes. Materials Women's shoes are often made with more flexible materials than men's shoes.

Men's Shoes Collection

Shop the latest in men's footwear.

Men's shoes are typically larger and wider than women's shoes, and are designed for durability and functionality. They come in a variety of styles, including casual and dress shoes. Men's shoe styles Oxfords: A classic dress shoe with a closed-throat lacing system Derbies: A lace-up shoe with an open-throat lacing system Monk straps: A shoe with a buckle closure instead of laces Loafers: A slip-on shoe with a wide heel Brogues: A style of Oxford, Blucher, or Loafer with a perforated design Chukka: A Derby-style ankle boot Desert boots: A type of Chukka Boat shoes: A low-key version of loafers Sneakers: A casual athletic shoe Sandals: An open-toe style with one or two straps.
